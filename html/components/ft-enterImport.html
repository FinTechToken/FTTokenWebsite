Import Account: Advanced Information

Importing Account let's you interface directly with blockchain without any information going to our servers. This is the most secure. But, it means a few things.

- We cannot recover your information. We don't store it, so we can't recover it.
- We cannot send you your account via email or phone. We don't store it, so we can't help you.
- You'll need to enter your Encrypted Account each time you visit the site. We don't store it so you need to enter it every time.
