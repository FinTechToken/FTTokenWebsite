(complete) Step 1) Create an account. Tutorial Step 4) Trade Free Token for Ether
Sell your 1 FreeToken to get Ether in your account.
- Press the minus button.
- Click the 0 number hyperlink and choose 1 Ether of tokens to sell
- Click the 0 price hyperlink and choose anything under 5 Ether.
(There is currently a buy order on the book for 5 Ether. If you sell for anything less than 5 you will get the 5 Ether that is offered on the book.)

(For fun you can sell for > 5 ether and your trade will go on the book. You can then cancel the offer to move it off the book.)
