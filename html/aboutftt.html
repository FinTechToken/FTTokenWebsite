We improve global access to capital, education and overall prosperity by


  1. Creating useful blockchain products/services.

  2. Making blockchain accessible and easy to use.

Team is experienced in finance, regulation, and technology


In 2010 we launched a registered investment advisory firm doing algorithmic trading.

In 2015 we created TuitionCoin, a licensed consumer finance company that refinances student loans. Our analytics brought the average lifetime default rate down to under 2%. Our technology brought loan costs down to 1/8th what it costs a traditional bank to do student loans.

In 2017 we launched FinTechToken to contribute to blockchain ecosystem.

Smart contracts drastically improve finance for everyone.


Our regulated consumer finance company, TuitionCoin, and our regulated registered investment advisory firm, eSmeb Inc, uses smart contracts we build, as proof that blockchain technology is more effective than traditional financial services.

Our first blockchain code is a token market, which enables trading directly on blockchain, without a 3rd party.
