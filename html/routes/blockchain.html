FTT - The FinTechToken Public Network


FTT is a Public Blockchain that connects with all major crypto currencies.

We aim to be a crypto sun giving life to other blockchains in our solar system. Ethereum and BitCoin are both in our gravitational pull. More will be pulled in soon.

FTT works just like Ethereum with a few exceptions.
- there is no need to pay for transactions. Gas is totally free.
- transactions happen every second. Instead of every 15 seconds
- The base currency is dollars making it easy to know what things costs.

FTT is on block {{!getBlocknumber() ? 0 : getBlocknumber() | number}}. We show the last 3 digits in the navigation bar at the top of your screen.

Transactions happen when a new block is mined, which should happen approximately every second.
