About Blockchain Accounts
What information is needed to create an account?
None. Blockchain revolutionizes privacy. You can use services without disclosing personal information.

How much does an account cost?
It's free.

What is a blockchain account?
An account is made up of a public address and a private key. Both are two long strings of charecters.

  • Public Address: This "address" is publicly viewed on the blockchain. If someone wants to send something to you, they use the public address.
  • Private Key: The key is used to encrypt/decrypt blockchain transactions. This must be kept secure because the holder of the key can use the account.

What is a PassPhrase?
The PassPhrase is similar to a password. It is used to encrypt your account. You use it when you sign in. We suggest using a phrase because 4-5 random words is easier for people to remember and almost impossible to crack.
